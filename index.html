About

Designing with purpose & intent.

I'm Avi — a self-taught web designer and creative based in Melbourne, Australia. My journey started the way most real ones do: not in a classroom, but out of necessity. A gaming community needed a website, and I figured it out.

I taught myself by pulling apart the web — inspecting how Apple, Microsoft, and other industry leaders built their interfaces. What started as curiosity became a craft, shaped over years of learning, stepping away, and always coming back — because it stopped being a hobby and became a passion.

Today I'm the founder of CodeDesignLab — a curated design ecosystem built for web designers, frontend developers, and indie founders. Everything I create is intentional, minimal, and built to make the people I work with look like they mean it.
